# Realty Media

## Purpose of This File

This README is the working context file for the Realty Media agency concept. It is written so future AI chats can quickly understand the business, strategic direction, offer structure, assumptions, and current decisions without needing the original conversation.

The company is now called **Realty Media** and uses **RM** as the short website mark.

## Simple Service Explanation

> We make real estate agents look premium online and turn every listing into Instagram content that brings seller trust.

## High-Level Concept

Realty Media is a real estate-focused media and marketing agency for agents in Romania, starting in **Cluj / Cluj-Napoca**.

The core idea is not just to sell photography or videography. The preferred positioning is a **full agent growth system**:

- High-quality real estate media production
- Instagram profile and content management
- Listing reels and highlight videos
- HDR real estate photography
- Drone footage
- Custom agent lead-capture websites
- Custom landing pages for individual listings
- FameScale-powered distribution and posting system
- AI-assisted automation behind the scenes

The agency should feel **performance-driven, trustworthy, and premium**, while still being accessible enough for the Romanian/Cluj real estate market.

## Current Strategic Direction

The team has decided to **steer away from 360 tours for now**.

The focus should instead be:

- HDR property photography using proper three-step photography
- Reels and short-form listing videos
- Highlight reels
- Drone media
- Strong editing and visual presentation
- Instagram management for agents
- Clean, premium, conversion-focused websites and landing pages

360 tours may be revisited later, but they are not part of the current core offer.

## Target Market

- Solo agents

The current belief is that agents personally are likely the best first target, before agencies.

### Secondary Audience

Secondary audiences include:

Airbnb and short-term rental owners are considered a later secondary offer, not the initial focus.

### Geography

Initial focus:

- Romania
- More specifically Cluj County / Cluj-Napoca

The agency should be shaped around the realities of the local Romanian real estate market, where many agents may not have large marketing budgets.

## Market Understanding

Romanian real estate discovery is still heavily portal-driven. Buyers commonly use portals such as:

- Storia
- OLX Imobiliare
- Imobiliare.ro
- Other local classifieds/real estate platforms

Because of this, the agency should not position itself as replacing portals. Instead, the stronger angle is that Realty Media helps agents look premium and trustworthy **outside the portals**, especially when sellers are deciding which agent to hire.

The likely buyer psychology:

- Buyers search listings on portals.
- Sellers choose agents based on trust, perceived competence, proof, presentation, and personal brand.
- Most agents have weak Instagram profiles, inconsistent visuals, bad listing media, and no strong personal brand.
- A premium-looking agent with strong media, visible proof, and active social content can appear more trustworthy and professional.

Recommended core angle:

> We make agents look premium outside the portals, so sellers choose them before they even call.

## Competitive Landscape

A light public competitive scan suggests that the Romanian/Cluj market already understands real estate media as a category. There are competitors offering property photography, videography, drone footage, 360/3D tours, mini-sites, and social-ready videos.

This means Realty Media is not inventing demand from zero. The category exists. The strategic task is to avoid sounding like another generic photo/video provider.

### Competitor Types

Common competitor buckets:

- Property photography and video freelancers
- Drone and aerial production providers
- Tourism/hospitality content agencies
- Full real estate media providers
- Premium real estate media studios outside Cluj, especially in Bucharest/Ilfov

Examples found during the scan:

- Mich Alin Media in Cluj: real estate photography and videography for agents, developers, owners, Airbnb, pensions, and hotels.
- Drone/aerial providers around Cluj: drone photography/video businesses serving real estate among other industries.
- DDM Studio in Cluj: tourism/hospitality-focused content and marketing, closer to the future Airbnb/cabana secondary offer.
- Case360: broader real estate/hospitality media including photo, 360, video, drone, website/admin, Google promotion, social promotion, and platform promotion.
- SpaceLens in Bucharest/Ilfov: a strong benchmark for premium real estate media, offering HDR photography, video, 3D tours, plans, mini-sites, and social-ready content.

### What Competitors Validate

Competitors validate that the market can understand and buy:

- HDR property photography
- Real estate videography
- Drone footage
- Video walkthroughs
- Social-ready vertical video
- 360/3D tours
- Property mini-sites
- Real estate-specific visual presentation

### Main Competitive Gap

Most competitors sell **property media**.

Realty Media should sell **agent positioning, seller trust, Instagram execution, and content distribution**.

A normal competitor says:

> We photograph and film your property.

Realty Media should say:

> We make you look like the agent sellers trust with premium properties, then turn each listing into a full Instagram content campaign.

The strongest differentiation is not the camera, drone, or editing alone. The strongest differentiation is the system:

- Agent profile overhaul
- Premium property media
- Listing reels
- Seller-facing content
- Listing landing pages
- Agent lead-capture page
- Instagram posting and management
- FameScale-powered content distribution
- AI-assisted production workflow

### Competitive Warning

Do not let the offer look like:

That would make the agency easy to compare on price and deliverables.

Instead, the agency should be positioned as a full agent trust and content system:

> A done-for-you content and trust system for real estate agents: premium listing media, Instagram management, and landing pages that make sellers take you seriously.

## Seller-Side Strategy

One important strategic principle: the agency should not only think about the buyer side of real estate marketing.

Property media helps sell or rent a listing, but agents may pay more when they believe the system helps them win future sellers. The money is often on the seller side because agents need property owners to trust them with serious listings.

Every case study should therefore show more than "this property got views." It should also communicate:

> This agent now looks like someone sellers would trust with a serious property.

This means the best content may be seller-facing, not only buyer-facing.

Examples of seller-facing content:

- Behind-the-scenes listing preparation
- Professional shoot process
- Before/after property presentation
- Sold-property proof
- Client/review highlights
- Agent expertise and market commentary
- "How I marketed this property" reels
- Case studies showing how a listing was presented across Instagram, landing pages, and portals

The agency should frame listing media as both:

- A way to market the current property
- A public proof asset that helps the agent win the next property owner

## Pricing Direction

The Romanian/Cluj market may not support very high retainers for average agents. Pricing should be accessible enough to win early clients, while still protecting the perceived value of the system.

Early possible pricing structure:

- Starter Listing Shoot: 100-200 EUR per property
- Agent Starter Retainer: 350-500 EUR/month
- Growth Retainer: 700-1,000 EUR/month
- Luxury / Agency Package: custom, potentially 1,500 EUR+

Current thinking:

- The team does not want to focus on per-listing pricing as the main model.
- Monthly retainers and package tiers are preferred.
- Per-property/listing work may still exist as a smaller entry offer.
- First case studies may be free or discounted, but should be framed as real value.

Important: If doing free work for case studies, present the normal value clearly. Example: "This would normally be a 700 EUR/month package."

Open question:

- `agent.realestatemediasystem.com`

Recommendation for early stage:

- Start with the agency's own domain/subdomain system for speed and control.
- Offer agent-owned domains later for premium clients.
